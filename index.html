Meet our newest faculty members

Ying Xu

As an assistant professor of education, Ying’s research focuses on designing AI technologies that promote language and literacy development, STEM learning, and wellbeing for children and families.

Learn more about her focus

Fiamma Straneo

Fiamma, professor of environmental science and engineering, is also a physical oceanographer and world leader in making field observations of melting Greenland glaciers to understand the complex relationship between ice, the ocean, and the atmosphere.

Learn more about her focus

Awa Ambra Seck

As an assistant professor of business, government and the international economy, Awa researches the intersection of development economics, economic history, and political economy, with a regional focus on Africa.

Learn more about her focus

Ishan Nath

As an assistant professor of public policy, Ishan explores the global economic impact of a warming climate.

Learn more about her focus

Briana Burris

As program director of the Oral and Maxillofacial Surgery Residency Training Program, Briana is excited to provide effective technical guidance during surgery.

Learn more about her focus

Shira Weingarten-Gabbay

Shira, assistant professor of microbiology, leads the new Laboratory of Systems Virology, where her team works at the intersection of virology, immunology, and systems biology.
